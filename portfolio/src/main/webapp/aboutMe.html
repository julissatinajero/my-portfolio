About Me

  • Julissa Tinajero

    • Hello! My name is Julissa Tinajero. I am the eldest of two daughters and a very proud Latina. I grew up in a small agricultural town located in central California where many immigrants, including my parents, found job opportunities working in the fields. My upbringing taught me the value of hard work, dedication, and persistance. Currently, I am attending San Jose State University studying Computer Science. I am the first in my family to go to college, and I'm passionate about succeeding in the tech field. My hope is to one day use my experiences to help other young girls like me pursue their own goals.
